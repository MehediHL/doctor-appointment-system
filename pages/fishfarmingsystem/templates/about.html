About Fish Farming Advisory System

Fish Farming Advisory System is a revolutionary platform designed to empower the fish farmers of Bangladesh with the power of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern technology, making aquaculture more sustainable, efficient, and profitable for everyone.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every fish farmer in Bangladesh has access to data-driven insights and expert advice at their fingertips. By leveraging cutting-edge AI, we aim to help increase yields, reduce losses, and promote environmentally friendly farming practices across the nation.

What We Offer

  • AI-Powered Fish Classification: Instantly identify fish species from an image and get tailored farming guides.
  • Expert Chatbot: Get immediate answers to your farming questions from our knowledgeable AI assistant, available 24/7.
  • Daily Suggestions: Receive personalized daily action plans for your specific fish culture, from feeding to water management.
  • Community Marketplace: A dedicated platform for farmers to buy and sell fish fry, connecting hatcheries directly with cultivators.
  • Data-Driven Planning: Utilize our tools for better budgeting and planning of your farming operations.

This project was built with a passion for technology and a deep respect for the hardworking farmers of Bangladesh. We are continuously working to improve our platform and add new features to better serve the aquaculture community.
